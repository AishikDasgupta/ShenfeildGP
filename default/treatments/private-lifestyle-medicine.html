Our Lifestyle Medicine Services

Comprehensive Health Transformation

We provide comprehensive lifestyle medicine services that address the root causes of health issues through evidence-based lifestyle interventions and medical support.

Weight Loss Clinic

Comprehensive weight management program combining medical supervision, nutrition counseling, and lifestyle coaching for sustainable weight loss.

What's Included:
  • Comprehensive health assessment
  • Personalized nutrition plan
  • Exercise prescription
  • Behavioral counseling
  • Medical weight loss medications
  • Ongoing support and monitoring
Duration: 60-90 minutes Program: 3-12 months

Diabetes Treatment

Comprehensive diabetes management including lifestyle interventions, medication optimization, and ongoing monitoring for optimal blood sugar control.

What's Included:
  • Diabetes education and counseling
  • Blood sugar monitoring
  • Medication management
  • Nutrition therapy
  • Exercise prescription
  • Complication prevention
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: Monthly

Migraine Treatment

Specialized migraine management including trigger identification, lifestyle modifications, and medical treatments to reduce frequency and severity.

What's Included:
  • Migraine assessment and diagnosis
  • Trigger identification
  • Lifestyle modifications
  • Medication management
  • Preventive treatments
  • Acute treatment strategies
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 4-8 weeks

General Lifestyle Coaching

Comprehensive lifestyle coaching to improve overall health, energy levels, and quality of life through sustainable lifestyle changes.

What's Included:
  • Health and wellness assessment
  • Nutrition counseling
  • Exercise prescription
  • Stress management
  • Sleep optimization
  • Habit formation support
Duration: 60-90 minutes Program: 3-6 months

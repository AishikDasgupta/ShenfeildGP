Our Mental Health Services

Professional Mental Health Support

We provide compassionate mental health support services including treatment for anxiety and depression with personalised care plans.

Anxiety Treatment

Comprehensive anxiety treatment including assessment, therapy, and medication management to help you manage anxiety and improve your quality of life.

What's Included:
  • Detailed anxiety assessment
  • Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT)
  • Medication prescriptions (if needed)
  • Stress management techniques
  • Coping strategies development
  • Regular follow-up appointments
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: 2-4 weeks

Depression Treatment

Professional depression treatment with personalised care plans including therapy and medication management to help you overcome depression.

What's Included:
  • Comprehensive depression screening
  • Personalised treatment plans
  • Antidepressant medication management
  • Therapeutic counselling
  • Progress monitoring
  • Ongoing support and care
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: 2-4 weeks

Immunotherapy Treatment

Long-term immunotherapy treatment to desensitize your immune system to specific allergens and reduce hay fever symptoms over time.

What's Included:
  • Immunotherapy consultation
  • Allergen extract preparation
  • Gradual dose escalation
  • Regular monitoring
  • Progress assessment
  • Long-term follow-up
Duration: 30-45 minutes Treatment: 3-5 years

Lifestyle & Prevention Advice

Comprehensive lifestyle advice and prevention strategies to minimise hay fever symptoms and reduce exposure to allergens.

What's Included:
  • Allergen avoidance strategies
  • Indoor air quality advice
  • Outdoor activity planning
  • Diet and nutrition guidance
  • Home environment optimization
  • Seasonal preparation tips
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: As needed

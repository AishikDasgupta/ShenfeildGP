Our Geriatric Care Services

Comprehensive Elderly Healthcare

We provide specialized geriatric care designed to address the unique health challenges faced by elderly patients, focusing on maintaining independence and quality of life.

Comprehensive Health Assessment

Thorough health assessment focusing on age-related conditions, medication management, and overall well-being of elderly patients.

What's Included:
  • Comprehensive medical history review
  • Medication review and optimization
  • Fall risk assessment
  • Cognitive function evaluation
  • Nutritional status assessment
  • Social support evaluation
Duration: 60-90 minutes Follow-up: 3-6 months

Chronic Disease Management

Specialized management of chronic conditions common in elderly patients including diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease.

What's Included:
  • Diabetes management and monitoring
  • Hypertension control
  • Heart disease management
  • Arthritis and joint care
  • Osteoporosis screening and treatment
  • Medication management
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 1-3 months

Memory and Cognitive Care

Specialized care for memory issues, dementia screening, and cognitive health support for elderly patients and their families.

What's Included:
  • Memory assessment and screening
  • Dementia evaluation
  • Cognitive function testing
  • Family support and counseling
  • Medication for cognitive issues
  • Referral to specialists if needed
Duration: 60-90 minutes Follow-up: 3-6 months

Mobility and Independence Support

Support for maintaining mobility, independence, and quality of life through physical therapy, assistive devices, and home safety assessments.

What's Included:
  • Mobility assessment
  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Home safety evaluation
  • Assistive device recommendations
  • Physical therapy referrals
  • Independence support planning
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 3-6 months

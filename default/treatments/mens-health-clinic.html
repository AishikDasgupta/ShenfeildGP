Our Men's Health Services

Comprehensive Care

We provide specialised men's health services with a focus on common male health concerns, delivered with discretion and expertise.

Erectile Dysfunction Treatment

Comprehensive assessment and treatment of erectile dysfunction with a range of effective treatment options tailored to your individual needs.

What We Offer:
  • Confidential consultation and assessment
  • Medical history and lifestyle evaluation
  • Physical examination and testing
  • Medication prescription and management
  • Lifestyle and psychological support
  • Follow-up care and monitoring
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 4-6 weeks

Prostate Screening

Comprehensive prostate health screening including PSA testing, digital rectal examination, and risk assessment for prostate cancer and other prostate conditions.

What We Offer:
  • PSA blood test and interpretation
  • Digital rectal examination (DRE)
  • Prostate cancer risk assessment
  • Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) screening
  • Urinary symptom evaluation
  • Follow-up care and referrals if needed
Duration: 30-45 minutes Results: Within 1 week

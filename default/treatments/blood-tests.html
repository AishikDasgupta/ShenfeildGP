Our Blood Testing Services

Comprehensive Diagnostic Testing

We provide a full range of blood testing services to monitor your health, diagnose conditions, and track treatment progress with fast, accurate results.

Routine Health Monitoring

Essential blood tests for routine health monitoring including complete blood count, cholesterol, and basic metabolic panel.

What's Included:
  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Cholesterol and Lipid Profile
  • Basic Metabolic Panel
  • Liver Function Tests
  • Kidney Function Tests
  • Thyroid Function Tests
Duration: 15-20 minutes Results: Within 24-48 hours

Diabetes Monitoring

Comprehensive diabetes monitoring including HbA1c, glucose tolerance tests, and diabetic complication screening.

What's Included:
  • HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)
  • Fasting Blood Glucose
  • Oral Glucose Tolerance Test
  • Random Blood Glucose
  • Diabetic Complication Screening
  • Insulin Level Testing
Duration: 20-30 minutes Results: Within 24-48 hours

Cardiovascular Risk Assessment

Comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessment including cholesterol, inflammatory markers, and heart health indicators.

What's Included:
  • Total Cholesterol and HDL/LDL
  • Triglycerides
  • C-Reactive Protein (CRP)
  • Homocysteine
  • Lipoprotein(a)
  • Cardiac Enzymes
Duration: 15-20 minutes Results: Within 24-48 hours

Specialised Testing

Specialised blood tests for specific conditions including hormone levels, vitamin deficiencies, and autoimmune markers.

What's Included:
  • Hormone Level Testing
  • Vitamin and Mineral Levels
  • Autoimmune Markers
  • Allergy Testing
  • Cancer Markers
  • Infectious Disease Screening
Duration: 20-30 minutes Results: 3-7 days

Our Blood Testing Process

What to Expect

Our blood testing process is designed to be quick, comfortable, and efficient, providing you with accurate results and clear interpretation.

1. Consultation

Discuss your testing needs and medical history with our healthcare team to determine the appropriate tests.

2. Sample Collection

Quick and professional blood sample collection by our trained phlebotomists in a comfortable setting.

3. Laboratory Analysis

Your samples are analyzed in our state-of-the-art laboratory using advanced diagnostic equipment.

4. Results & Review

Receive your results promptly with expert interpretation and recommendations from our medical team.

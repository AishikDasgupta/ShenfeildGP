Our Hay Fever Treatment Services

Comprehensive Allergy Care

We provide comprehensive hay fever treatment and allergy management services to help you find relief from seasonal allergies and enjoy the outdoors comfortably.

Allergy Assessment & Testing

Comprehensive allergy assessment to identify specific allergens causing your hay fever symptoms and develop targeted treatment plans.

What's Included:
  • Detailed allergy history review
  • Skin prick testing
  • Blood allergy testing
  • Pollen count monitoring
  • Allergen identification
  • Severity assessment
Duration: 45-60 minutes Results: Within 1 week

Medication Management

Expert prescription and management of antihistamines, nasal sprays, and other medications to effectively control hay fever symptoms.

What's Included:
  • Antihistamine prescriptions
  • Nasal corticosteroid sprays
  • Eye drop medications
  • Decongestant management
  • Medication optimization
  • Side effect monitoring
Duration: 20-30 minutes Follow-up: 2-4 weeks

Immunotherapy Treatment

Long-term immunotherapy treatment to desensitize your immune system to specific allergens and reduce hay fever symptoms over time.

What's Included:
  • Immunotherapy consultation
  • Allergen extract preparation
  • Gradual dose escalation
  • Regular monitoring
  • Progress assessment
  • Long-term follow-up
Duration: 30-45 minutes Treatment: 3-5 years

Lifestyle & Prevention Advice

Comprehensive lifestyle advice and prevention strategies to minimize hay fever symptoms and reduce exposure to allergens.

What's Included:
  • Allergen avoidance strategies
  • Indoor air quality advice
  • Outdoor activity planning
  • Diet and nutrition guidance
  • Home environment optimization
  • Seasonal preparation tips
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: As needed

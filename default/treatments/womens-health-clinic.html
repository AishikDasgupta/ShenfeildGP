Our Women's Health Services

Comprehensive Care

We provide a full range of women's health services, from routine gynecological care to specialized treatments, all delivered with compassion and expertise.

Coil Insertion/Removal

Professional coil (IUD) insertion and removal services with experienced practitioners. We offer both hormonal and copper coil options to suit your contraceptive needs.

What We Offer:
  • Pre-insertion consultation and assessment
  • Hormonal coil (Mirena, Kyleena) insertion
  • Copper coil (ParaGard) insertion
  • Coil removal and replacement
  • Follow-up care and monitoring
  • Contraceptive counseling and advice
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: 6 weeks

Menopause & HRT Treatment

Comprehensive menopause management including hormone replacement therapy (HRT) consultation, prescription, and ongoing monitoring for optimal symptom relief.

What We Offer:
  • Menopause symptom assessment
  • Hormone level testing
  • HRT consultation and prescription
  • Bone density assessment
  • Lifestyle and nutrition advice
  • Ongoing monitoring and adjustment
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 3 months

Private Smear Test

Private cervical screening services with quick results and expert interpretation. We provide a comfortable, professional environment for this important health check.

What We Offer:
  • Private cervical screening (smear test)
  • HPV testing and co-testing
  • Quick results (within 1 week)
  • Expert result interpretation
  • Follow-up care if needed
  • Comfortable, private consultation
Duration: 15-20 minutes Results: Within 1 week

UTI Treatment

Expert diagnosis and treatment of urinary tract infections with same-day appointments available for urgent cases. We provide comprehensive UTI management and prevention advice.

What We Offer:
  • Urgent UTI diagnosis and treatment
  • Urine testing and culture
  • Antibiotic prescription if needed
  • Recurrent UTI management
  • Prevention strategies and advice
  • Follow-up care and monitoring
Duration: 20-30 minutes Same-day appointments available

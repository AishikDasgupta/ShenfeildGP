Our Children's Health Services

Comprehensive Paediatric Care

We provide a full range of children's health services designed to support your child's growth and development from infancy through adolescence.

Routine Child Health Checks

Regular health check-ups to monitor your child's growth, development, and overall health from birth through teenage years.

What's Included:
  • Growth and development assessment
  • Physical examination
  • Developmental milestone tracking
  • Nutrition and feeding advice
  • Safety and injury prevention
  • Parenting support and guidance
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: As recommended

Childhood Vaccinations

Complete vaccination services following the NHS schedule and additional vaccines for travel and specific health needs.

What's Included:
  • NHS vaccination schedule
  • Travel vaccinations
  • Flu vaccinations for children
  • Meningitis vaccinations
  • HPV vaccinations
  • Vaccination catch-up programs
Duration: 15-30 minutes Follow-up: As per schedule

Acute Childhood Illness

Prompt diagnosis and treatment of common childhood illnesses including infections, fevers, and minor injuries.

What's Included:
  • Fever and infection management
  • Respiratory infections
  • Ear, nose, and throat problems
  • Skin conditions and rashes
  • Minor injuries and cuts
  • Emergency care when needed
Duration: 20-30 minutes Same-day appointments available

Developmental Assessment

Comprehensive assessment of your child's physical, cognitive, and emotional development with early intervention support.

What's Included:
  • Developmental milestone assessment
  • Speech and language evaluation
  • Motor skills development
  • Behavioral assessment
  • Learning difficulties screening
  • Referral to specialists if needed
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 3-6 months

Our Children's Health Process

What to Expect

Our children's health services are designed to be child-friendly, comprehensive, and reassuring, providing expert care for your child's health and development.

1. Initial Consultation

Comprehensive discussion of your child's health, development, medical history, and any concerns with our paediatric care team.

2. Child-Friendly Examination

Gentle, age-appropriate physical examination conducted in a comfortable, child-friendly environment to put children at ease.

3. Vaccinations & Testing

Child vaccinations, developmental assessments, and any necessary diagnostic tests performed with care and sensitivity.

4. Care Plan & Support

Personalised care plan, health recommendations, and ongoing support to ensure your child's continued health and development.

Our Pain Management Services

Comprehensive Pain Relief

We provide comprehensive pain management services designed to address both acute and chronic pain conditions with personalized treatment approaches.

Knee Pain Treatment

Specialized treatment for knee pain including arthritis, injuries, and degenerative conditions with comprehensive pain management approaches.

What's Included:
  • Knee pain assessment and diagnosis
  • Imaging and diagnostic tests
  • Physical therapy referrals
  • Pain medication management
  • Injection therapies
  • Lifestyle modifications
Duration: 45-60 minutes Follow-up: 2-4 weeks

Chronic Pain Management

Comprehensive management of chronic pain conditions including back pain, neuropathic pain, and fibromyalgia with multidisciplinary approaches.

What's Included:
  • Chronic pain assessment
  • Pain medication optimization
  • Physical therapy coordination
  • Psychological support
  • Alternative therapies
  • Pain management planning
Duration: 60-90 minutes Follow-up: Monthly

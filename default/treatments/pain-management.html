Knee Pain Treatment

Comprehensive knee pain assessment and treatment including injections, physical therapy guidance, and pain management strategies.

What's Included:
  • Detailed knee examination
  • Pain assessment and diagnosis
  • Knee joint injections (if needed)

Minor Surgery for Skin Lesions

Professional minor surgery services for the removal of skin lesions including moles, cysts, and other benign growths.

What's Included:
  • Pre-surgery consultation
  • Local anesthesia
  • Lesion removal procedure
  • Wound care and dressing
  • Histology if required
  • Post-operative follow-up
Duration: 30-60 minutes Follow-up: 1-2 weeks

Travel Clinic

Comprehensive travel health services including vaccinations, health advice, and travel medicine consultations for international travelers.

What's Included:
  • Travel health consultation
  • Destination-specific advice
  • Malaria prophylaxis prescription
  • Travel health kit recommendations
  • Disease prevention guidance
  • Fitness to travel assessment
Duration: 30-45 minutes Book: 4-6 weeks before travel

Our Pain Management Process

What to Expect

Our pain management process is designed to be comprehensive, effective, and personalised, providing you with relief from pain and improved quality of life.

1. Pain Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your pain, its location, intensity, and impact on your daily activities and quality of life.

2. Diagnosis & Evaluation

Thorough examination and diagnostic tests to identify the underlying cause of your pain and determine the best treatment approach.

3. Treatment Plan

Personalised treatment plan including medications, injections, physical therapy, or minor surgical procedures as appropriate.

4. Ongoing Management

Regular follow-up to monitor progress, adjust treatment, and provide ongoing support for long-term pain relief.

Our Cardiovascular Health Services

Heart Health Monitoring

We provide comprehensive cardiovascular health services to monitor your heart health, assess risk factors, and provide personalised care to maintain optimal cardiovascular function.

ECG Testing

Electrocardiogram (ECG) testing to assess your heart's electrical activity and detect any abnormalities or irregularities.

What's Included:
  • Resting ECG
  • Exercise ECG (Stress Test)
  • 24-Hour Holter Monitoring
  • ECG Interpretation
  • Follow-up Recommendations
  • Referral to Specialists if Needed
Duration: 15-30 minutes Results: Immediate

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Comprehensive blood pressure monitoring and management to help control hypertension and reduce cardiovascular risk.

What's Included:
  • Blood Pressure Measurement
  • 24-Hour Ambulatory Monitoring
  • Home Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Hypertension Management
  • Lifestyle Counselling
  • Medication Management
Duration: 20-30 minutes Follow-up: 2-4 weeks

Cardiovascular Risk Assessment

Comprehensive assessment of your cardiovascular risk factors including family history, lifestyle, and medical conditions.

What's Included:
  • Cardiovascular Risk Calculator
  • Family History Assessment
  • Lifestyle Risk Factors
  • Cholesterol and Lipid Testing
  • Diabetes Screening
  • Personalised Risk Reduction Plan
Duration: 45-60 minutes Results: Within 1 week

Heart Health Counselling

Personalised heart health counselling including lifestyle modifications, diet recommendations, and exercise planning.

What's Included:
  • Heart-Healthy Diet Counselling
  • Exercise Recommendations
  • Stress Management Techniques
  • Smoking Cessation Support
  • Weight Management
  • Medication Adherence Support
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: 3-6 months

Our Cardiovascular Health Process

What to Expect

Our cardiovascular health assessment process is designed to be thorough, comfortable, and informative, providing you with comprehensive heart health evaluation and clear recommendations.

1. Initial Assessment

Complete cardiovascular risk assessment including medical history, lifestyle factors, and family history review.

2. Physical Examination

Comprehensive cardiovascular examination including blood pressure, heart rate, and heart rhythm assessment.

3. Diagnostic Testing

ECG testing, blood tests, and other diagnostic tests as appropriate to assess your heart health comprehensively.

4. Results & Plan

Detailed results discussion, personalised heart health recommendations, and ongoing monitoring plan.

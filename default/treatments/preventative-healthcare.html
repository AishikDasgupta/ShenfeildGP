Our Preventative Healthcare Process

What to Expect

Our preventative healthcare process is designed to be comprehensive, proactive, and personalized, helping you maintain optimal health and prevent future health issues.

1. Health Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your current health status, risk factors, and lifestyle factors affecting your well-being.

2. Screening & Testing

Age and risk-appropriate health screenings, blood tests, and diagnostic evaluations to identify potential health issues early.

3. Prevention Plan

Personalized prevention plan including lifestyle recommendations, vaccination schedules, and health optimization strategies.

4. Ongoing Monitoring

Regular follow-up appointments and health monitoring to track progress and adjust prevention strategies as needed.

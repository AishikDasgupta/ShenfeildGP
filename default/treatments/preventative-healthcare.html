Our Preventative Healthcare Services

Proactive Health Management

We provide comprehensive preventative healthcare services including dermatology treatment and corporate health screening to keep you healthy and well.

Dermatology Treatment

Comprehensive dermatology services including skin assessments, treatment of skin conditions, and preventative skin care consultations.

What's Included:
  • Full skin examination
  • Mole and lesion assessment
  • Acne treatment and management
  • Eczema and psoriasis care
  • Skin cancer screening
  • Prescription dermatology medications
Duration: 30-45 minutes Follow-up: As needed

Corporate Health Screening

Comprehensive corporate health screening programs designed for businesses to ensure employee wellness and early detection of health issues.

What's Included:
  • Executive health assessments
  • Fitness to work evaluations
  • Health risk assessments
  • Blood pressure and BMI checks
  • Blood tests and health markers
  • Detailed health reports
Duration: 45-60 minutes Annual screening recommended

Routine Health Monitoring

Essential preventative healthcare for routine health monitoring including comprehensive blood tests and health assessments.

What's Included:
  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Cholesterol and Lipid Profile
  • Basic Metabolic Panel
  • Liver Function Tests
  • Kidney Function Tests
  • Thyroid Function Tests
Duration: 15-20 minutes Results: Within 24-48 hours

Health Risk Assessment

Specialized preventative healthcare for specific conditions including hormone levels, vitamin deficiencies, and autoimmune markers.

What's Included:
  • Hormone Level Testing
  • Vitamin and Mineral Levels
  • Autoimmune Markers
  • Allergy Testing
  • Cancer Markers
  • Infectious Disease Screening
Duration: 20-30 minutes Results: 3-7 days

How to Reach Us

Transportation Options

We're easily accessible by various means of transport. Choose the option that works best for you.

By Car

Free parking available on-site. Located on High Street with easy access from A3 and M25.

  • From London: Take A3 southbound
  • From Guildford: Take A3 northbound
  • Free parking spaces available

By Train

Shenfeild Station is just 5 minutes walk from our practice.

  • Direct trains from London Waterloo
  • 5-minute walk from station
  • Regular service every 30 minutes

By Bus

Multiple bus routes stop directly outside our practice.

  • Bus routes: 123, 456, 789
  • Stop: High Street Medical Centre
  • Frequent service throughout day

On Foot

Located in the town center, easily accessible by foot.

  • 5 minutes from town center
  • Wheelchair accessible entrance
  • Well-lit pedestrian routes

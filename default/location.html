How to Reach Us

Transportation Options

We are conveniently located on Hutton Road, just a short walk from Shenfield Station.

By Car

Easy access from A12 and M25 (Junction 28). Parking available nearby.

  • Friars Avenue Car Park (CM15 8JA)
  • Hunter Avenue Car Park (CM15 8PF)
  • On-street parking (restrictions apply)

By Train

Shenfield Station is just a 5-7 minutes walk from our practice.

  • Elizabeth Line & Greater Anglia
  • Direct from London Liverpool St.
  • Exit station, turn right onto Hutton Rd

By Bus

Several bus routes stop directly on Hutton Road near the practice.

  • Routes: 9, 80A, 80C, 81, 251, 339
  • Stop: Shenfield Station / Hutton Road
  • Frequent services available

On Foot

Located on the main high street, easily accessible by foot.

  • First Floor, 40 Hutton Road
  • Above the dental practice
  • Entrance clearly marked

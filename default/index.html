Dr Kannan Athreya

MBBS, DRCOG, DipDerm (Distinction)
Key Interests: Family Medicine, Skin Surgery, Aesthetics
Appointments: GP Consultations, Skin Assessments, Minor Surgeries.

With 30 years of experience serving the local community, Dr Kannan Athreya is back in Shenfield, providing dedicated private GP care to his patients.

Dr Athreya is a highly experienced local General Practitioner, having served Brentwood for thirty years. After 18 years as an NHS Partner at Mount Avenue Surgery, he commenced work as a Private GP in 2014, and is now delighted to be back in Shenfield working at Shenfield Private GP.

Dr Athreya believes in taking time to truly understand his patients as individuals, their circumstances, and what matters most to them, allowing care to be tailored in a thoughtful and meaningful way. Whilst all aspects of Family Practice are at the core of Dr Athreya's expertise, he is particularly passionate about supporting patients experiencing mental health challenges such as anxiety, depression, stress, and trauma. Dr Athreya finds it deeply rewarding to help people navigate difficult periods in their lives and work towards improved wellbeing, both personally and professionally.

Alongside this, Dr Athreya has extensive experience in dermatology, aesthetics, and minor surgery. He holds a Diploma in Dermatology with distinction and a Professional BTEC Award in Laser and Aesthetic Therapies. His clinical work ranges from managing chronic skin conditions in children, such as eczema, to the careful assessment and removal of benign skin lesions and cysts, always with comfort and safety at the forefront.

Outside of clinical practice, Dr Athreya enjoys an active lifestyle, including marathon running, climbing, and keeping fit. He believes that happiness and health are closely linked and strives to support his patients in leading healthy, balanced, and fulfilling lives.

Dr Ayub Khan

MBBS, MSc, MRCGP, DipPracDerm, PGCert Aesthetic Medicine, MSc Dermoscopy & Preventative Dermato-Oncology
Key Interests: Dermatology, Skin Cancer Detection, Mole Mapping, Aesthetic Medicine
Appointments: Full-body Mole Checks, Skin Consultations, GP Appointments

NHS GP and founder of Aesthetk, internationally recognised expert in dermoscopy with advanced qualifications in skin cancer detection.

Dr Ayub Khan is an experienced NHS General Practitioner with a special interest in Dermatology and is the founder of Aesthetk. Known to many patients as Dr K, he combines evidence-based medicine with advanced skin expertise to deliver high-quality, personalised care.

Dr Khan is an International Academic Expert in Dermoscopy, he holds a Master's degree in Dermoscopy and Preventative Dermato-Oncology from the Medical University of Graz, Austria, alongside postgraduate qualifications from Queen Mary University of London and Cardiff University. His specialist knowledge allows him to provide comprehensive skin assessments, including meticulous full-body digital mole mapping for the early detection and monitoring of skin cancer.

At Shenfield Private GP, Dr Khan is committed to a patient-centred approach, ensuring patients feel listened to, informed, and involved in decisions about their care. He is known for his attention to detail, clear explanations, and calm, reassuring manner. By integrating advanced diagnostic techniques with holistic general practice, he supports patients in maintaining long-term skin health and overall wellbeing.

MBBS, MRCGP, RCOG, DipDerm
Key Interests: Women's Health, Menopause, Obstetrics, Gynaecology, Dermatology
Appointments: Women's Health Consultations, Menopause Reviews, Skin Assessments

NHS GP specialising in Women's Health and Menopause, with Royal College memberships in both General Practice and Obstetrics & Gynaecology.

Dr Nana Gvazava is an NHS General Practitioner with a special interest in Women's Health and Menopause, supported by a strong background in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. She is a Member of both the Royal College of General Practitioners and the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists, reflecting her broad and in-depth clinical expertise.

Dr Nana also holds advanced qualifications in Dermatology and Dermoscopy from Cardiff University, enabling her to manage a wide range of skin concerns alongside her women's health practice. She is well known for her compassionate, holistic approach, focusing on understanding the underlying causes of symptoms rather than simply treating them in isolation.

She lectures at Anglia Ruskin University and contributes to the development of clinical guidelines, ensuring her practice remains current and evidence-based. Book Your Appointment

Easy Online Scheduling
  • How do I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment by calling us directly, using our online booking form, or visiting our practice. We offer same-day appointments for urgent needs and advance booking for routine check-ups.

  • What should I bring to my appointment?

    Please bring a valid photo ID, proof of address, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from other providers.

  • Do you offer urgent appointments?

    Yes, we reserve same-day appointment slots for urgent medical needs. Please call us early in the morning to secure an urgent appointment, or use our emergency contact number after hours.

  • How to find us?

    We are located on the first floor of the building.We do not have wheelchair access, Please contact the clinic to make separate arrangements.

  • Is the clinic wheelchair accessible?

    At present, the building does not have wheelchair access. We acknowledge that this may limit access for some patients and we sincerely apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

  • What should I do if I have mobility or other accessibility needs?

    We encourage patients to contact the clinic in advance of their appointment to discuss any mobility, sensory, or other accessibility requirements. Our team will make every reasonable effort to explore suitable adjustments or alternative arrangements where possible.

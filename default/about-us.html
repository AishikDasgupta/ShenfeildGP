Our Story

Dr Kannan Athreya

MBBS DRCOG DipDerm (Distinction)

I really value the traditional family doctor relationship I have with patients and their families. I take time to get the know the person and what's important to them as an individual.

I have a passion for helping people who may be experiencing depression, anxiety, trauma, and stress, that is impacting their lives. It is very rewarding to help people navigate through a difficult time to a better place, and to see the benefits that it brings not only the person, but also in their work and close relationships.

I have extensive experience in dermatology, aesthetics and minor surgery (completing a Diplomas in Dermatology, and I hold a Professional BTEC Award for Laser and Aesthetic Therapies). I really enjoy helping people to literally be more comfortable in their own skin, be it looking after a young child with persistent eczema, through to carefully removing an uncomfortable epidermal cyst.

Outside of Clinic life, I love to be outside – running marathons, climbing, and keeping fit. Happiness is everything to me, and it's my goal for my patients to lead healthy and happy, fulfilling lives.

Dr. Ayub Khan (Founder)

MBBS MSc MRCGP DipPracDerm PGCertAesthMed MSc DermPrevOncol

Dr. Ayub Khan, fondly known as Dr. K, is the founder and lead practitioner at Aesthetk. With a distinguished career as an NHS General Practitioner and a Special Interest (GPwSI) in Dermatology, Dr. K combines medical precision with aesthetic expertise.

Holding an MSc in Dermoscopy and Preventative Dermato-oncology from the prestigious Medical University of Graz, Austria, and additional qualifications from Queen Mary University of London and Cardiff University, he brings unparalleled knowledge to the field. Renowned for his meticulous full-body digital mole mapping and patient-centred care, Dr. K seamlessly blends innovative skin health monitoring with transformative aesthetic treatments.

Dr. Nana Gvazava

MBBS MRCGP RCOG DipDerm

Dr. Nana, an NHS GP with a special interest in Women's Health and Menopause, brings extensive expertise from her years in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. A member of the Royal College of General Practitioners (MRCGP) and the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists (RCOG), she also holds advanced qualifications in Dermatology and Dermoscopy from Cardiff University.

Dr. Nana is renowned for her compassionate, holistic approach, addressing the root causes of women's health concerns and tailoring treatments to individual needs. A dedicated educator and researcher, she also lectures at Anglia Ruskin University and actively contributes to advancing medical guidelines.

1. Introduction

Shenfield Private GP ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is Aesthetk Ltd, operating as Shenfield Private GP, registered at First Floor, 40 Hutton Road, Shenfield, Brentwood, Essex CM15 8LB.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Identity Data: Name, date of birth, gender.
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone numbers, postal address.
  • Health Data: Medical history, symptoms, treatment records, prescriptions.
  • Financial Data: Payment card details, billing information.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, cookies (see our Cookie Policy).

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract: To provide agreed healthcare services.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with CQC regulations and NHS requirements.
  • Vital Interests: In medical emergencies.
  • Consent: For marketing communications (where applicable).

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide medical services, consultations, and healthcare.
  • Manage appointments and process payments.
  • Communicate with you regarding your care and follow-ups.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Share relevant information with other healthcare providers (with your consent).

6. Data Retention

We retain your medical records in accordance with NHS guidelines (typically 10 years for adults, longer for children). Financial records are kept for 7 years as required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS).
  • Secure server storage with access controls.
  • Staff training on data protection.
  • Regular security audits.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Subject Access Request).
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of your data (where applicable).
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: contact@shenfieldprivategp.com
  • Phone: 01277 503 503
  • Address: Shenfield Private GP, First Floor, 40 Hutton Road, Shenfield, Brentwood, Essex CM15 8LB
